About Khaabar Adda

10 Years of
Experience

The Finest Dining Experience Since 2015

Founded by Chef Vikash Prasad in 2015, Khaabar Adda has established itself as one of the premier dining destinations in the city. Our philosophy is simple: source the finest ingredients, prepare them with skill and passion, and present them with artistry.

We take pride in our seasonal menu that changes monthly to reflect the best produce available. Our wine cellar boasts over 500 selections from around the world, carefully curated by our sommelier to complement our dishes.
